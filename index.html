About Us

Founded by Jermaine Thompson, a Bahamian with over 10 years of experience in pest control, landscaping, and property maintenance, Thompson's Pest Control brings local knowledge and scientific expertise to every job.

Our team is committed to providing environmentally-sensitive solutions that target specific pests while protecting the beneficial wildlife, pets, and delicate ecosystems that make the Bahamas so special.

Whether you're a large resort, government building, or private homeowner, we have the flexibility and expertise to handle jobs of any size throughout Eleuthera and accessible surrounding islands.
